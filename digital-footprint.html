Your Digital Footprint

Everything you do online leaves a trail — make it a positive one.

What is a Digital Footprint?

Your digital footprint is the collection of all traces you leave behind when using the internet — posts, likes, emails, and more. It includes both active data you choose to share and passive data collected automatically.

Risks

  • Identity theft due to oversharing
  • Unwanted marketing and spam
  • Negative impact on college/job applications

Advantages

  • Professional networking visibility
  • Online portfolio showcasing skills
  • Personal brand building

Positive Trail

Post helpful, truthful, and respectful content.

Negative Trail

Avoid harmful content that can damage your reputation.

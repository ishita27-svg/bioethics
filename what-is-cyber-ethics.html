What is Cyber Ethics?

The moral guidelines for behaviour in the digital world.

Definition

Cyber ethics refers to the study of how technology users should behave when using computers, the internet, and digital devices. It’s about respecting others online, protecting privacy, and using resources responsibly.

Scope & Importance

  • Individual Responsibility: How you safeguard your data and avoid unethical activities.
  • Corporate Responsibility: How companies protect customer data and maintain integrity.
  • Global Impact: Ethical practices contribute to a safer, fairer online environment worldwide.

Respect Privacy

Never access or share private information without permission.

Be Honest

Always present truthful information online.

Respect Others

Engage in polite, constructive discussions online.

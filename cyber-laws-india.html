Cyber Laws in India

Legal measures protecting online users and data integrity.

The IT Act 2000

The Information Technology Act, 2000 is India’s primary law to deal with cybercrime and electronic commerce. It covers issues like electronic signatures, digital record safety, and punishment for offences committed online.

Important Sections

Section Description Punishment
Section 66 Hacking of computer systems Up to 3 years imprisonment or fine
Section 66C Identity theft Up to 3 years imprisonment and fine up to ₹1 lakh
Section 67 Publishing obscene material online Up to 5 years imprisonment and fine

Cyberbullying Awareness

Recognise, prevent, and combat online harassment.

Types of Cyberbullying

  • Harassment through messages or posts
  • Spreading rumours and false information
  • Excluding someone from online groups

Signs

  • Sudden reluctance to use devices
  • Withdrawal from friends and family
  • Changes in mood or behaviour

Prevention

  • Talk openly with children about online safety.
  • Encourage them to report incidents.
  • Monitor their online activity.

Helplines

If you or someone you know is experiencing cyberbullying, contact:
National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Childline India Helpline: 1098

Stand Up

Don’t be a bystander – report cyberbullying incidents.

Report and Block

Take immediate action to prevent further harm.

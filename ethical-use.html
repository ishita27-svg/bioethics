Ethical Use of the Internet

Guiding principles for responsible online behaviour.

Do’s

  • Respect copyright laws.
  • Verify information before sharing.
  • Protect personal data.
  • Be polite and constructive in online discussions.

Don’ts

  • Engage in cyberbullying or harassment.
  • Spread misinformation or fake news.
  • Download or share pirated content.
  • Access others' accounts without permission.

Examples for Students

  • Do: Cite sources properly in research projects.
  • Don’t: Copy and paste content without attribution.
  • Do: Report suspicious activity to a trusted adult.
  • Don’t: Share inappropriate content with peers.

Take Responsibility

Own your actions and words online.

Act with Integrity

Be honest and ethical in all online interactions.

Stay Safe Online

Practical tips to protect yourself from cyber threats.

Passwords

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • Include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  • Consider a password manager to store them securely.

Privacy

  • Review and adjust privacy settings on social media.
  • Be mindful of what you share online.
  • Use secure messaging apps with end-to-end encryption.

Browsing

  • Use a secure browser with ad-blocking features.
  • Check for "https" in the address bar before entering sensitive information.
  • Be wary of suspicious links or attachments.

Welcome to Cyber Ethics

In today’s digital age, our lives are deeply intertwined with technology. Every click, search, message, and online interaction shapes the way we communicate, learn, and share information. As digital citizens, it is essential for us to understand how to use the internet responsibly and respectfully. “Understanding Cyber Ethics” by Ishita explores the principles that guide ethical behaviour in the online world. It highlights the importance of making thoughtful choices, respecting the privacy and rights of others, and staying safe while navigating the digital landscape. This introduction aims to help readers develop awareness about their online actions and encourages them to contribute to a safer, more trustworthy cyber environment.
